Thrive Education Financial Aid

Making Quality Education Accessible to All.Through financial and academic support,we aim to produce graduates with comprehensive skills who can secure excellent jobs and bring positive transfomation to the world.

Aim of the financial Aid

The Aim of our financial Aid Program is to make the courses offered and skills training done in the Thrive Education Centre accessible to people who would otherwise miss out opportunities due to current financial constraints.With the presence of Student Financing,we can help you navigate around your fees so you can concentrate on your education.This includes:
  • Low-Interest Loans
    • The school is partnerning with financial institutions to offer low interest loans specifically designed for students.
  • Scholarships
    • We are giving scholarships and grants to students based on financial need and Academic performance.
  • Flexible payment plans
    • We are also offering flexible payment plans that allows fees to be paid in installments.
